About Platypus

Platypus is an international travel app that is a one-stop source for important information and steps you need to travel abroad. This app will let you input a destination and immediately get information on type of visa needed, immunizations needed, and other requirements for travel. Registered users can then save this information as a checklist and add custom tasks.
